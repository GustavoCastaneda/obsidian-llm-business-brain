# Skill Registry

## Local Skills

- `business-vault-maintainer` - maintain MOCs, index, logs, links, and vault health.
- `business-call-ingest` - convert meeting notes into source notes and business synthesis.
- `business-email-ingest` - convert email threads into source notes and business synthesis.
- `business-founder-interview` - ask founder questions and file answers into the right business pages.
- `business-decision-capture` - turn decisions into durable decision notes and register entries.
- `business-investor-intel` - process investor calls and fundraising intelligence.
- `business-customer-intel` - process customer calls and customer intelligence.
- `business-finance-strategist` - analyze pricing, business model, unit economics, setup fees, SaaS consumption model, and fundraising assumptions using vault context.

## Promotion Rule

Keep skills local until they have been used at least twice in real work. Then install or copy the stable version into your agent's global skills directory.
