# Operating Rules

Use these rules for day-to-day maintenance of the Business Brain.

1. Treat `01-Sources/` as non-destructive evidence.
2. Update existing synthesis pages after meaningful ingests.
3. Append important ingests, decisions, structural changes, and maintenance passes to `log.md`.
4. Propose newly detected actions before adding them to `02-Business/Operations/Action Tracker.md`, unless the user explicitly asks to track them.
5. Ask targeted questions instead of inventing missing business facts.
6. Periodically flag orphan pages, duplicate concepts, unused folders, stale claims, contradictions, and source notes not integrated into business pages.
7. Recommend cleanup before adding structure when the vault has overlapping pages or unused folders.
