# Business Interview

Use this skill when company context is missing and the founder can provide the source of truth.

## Workflow

1. Identify the business area with missing context.
2. Ask one focused question at a time.
3. Convert the answer into durable synthesis in `02-Business/`.
4. Add open questions rather than guessing.
5. Update affected MOCs and `index.md`.
6. Append a query or interview entry to `log.md`.

## Priority Topics

- company overview
- current strategy
- product workflow
- ICP
- GTM motion
- fundraising narrative
- technical architecture
- operating cadence
