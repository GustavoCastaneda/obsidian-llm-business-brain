# Business Decision Capture

Use this skill when a decision is made, discovered in a meeting, or reconstructed from prior context.

## Workflow

1. Create or update a decision note in `02-Business/Decisions/`.
2. Capture decision, date, context, options, rationale, owner, expected consequences, and source links.
3. Update `02-Business/Decisions/Decision Register.md`.
4. Link the decision from affected business pages.
5. Append a decision entry to `log.md`.

## Rule

If the decision is ambiguous, record the ambiguity explicitly and ask for confirmation before presenting it as final.
