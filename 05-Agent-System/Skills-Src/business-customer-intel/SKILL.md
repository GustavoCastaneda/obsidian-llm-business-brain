# Customer Intel

## Shared Rules

Before creating files, changing structure, or preserving content, read the relevant files in `05-Agent-System/Rules/`.

Use this skill for customer calls, prospect calls, discovery notes, and user research.

## Workflow

1. Read customer source notes and related business pages.
2. Extract pain points, use cases, current workaround, buyer/user roles, urgency, objections, and product feedback.
3. Update `02-Business/Customers/ICP.md`.
4. Update `02-Business/Product/Product Overview.md` when product implications are clear.
5. Update `02-Business/GTM/GTM Strategy.md` and `02-Business/Sales/Sales Pipeline Notes.md` when GTM implications are clear.
6. Update [[MOC - Customers]], [[MOC - Product]], and [[MOC - GTM]].
7. Append an entry to `log.md`.

## Rule

Separate direct customer language from synthesis. Quote sparingly and link back to the source note.
