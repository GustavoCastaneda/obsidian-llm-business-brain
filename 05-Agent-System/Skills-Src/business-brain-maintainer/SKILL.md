# Business Brain Maintainer

Use this skill when maintaining the Obsidian Business Brain vault as a persistent company wiki.

## Workflow

1. Read `AGENTS.md`, `index.md`, and the relevant MOC before editing.
2. Keep source notes in `01-Sources/` evidentiary and non-destructive.
3. Integrate durable synthesis into `02-Business/`.
4. Update all affected MOCs in `03-MOCs/`.
5. Update `index.md` when a meaningful page or collection is added.
6. Append a parseable entry to `log.md`.

## Health Checks

Check for broken links, orphan pages, missing frontmatter, source notes not linked from business pages, MOCs missing new pages, stale claims, and contradictory claims.
