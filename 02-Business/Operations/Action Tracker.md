# Action Tracker

## How to Use

This is the central board for active business follow-ups. Account pages, legal notes, source notes, and meeting/email summaries keep the detail and evidence. Update status, owner, and due date here when a commitment changes.

The agent may detect possible actions while reading the vault, but it should ask for confirmation before adding them here unless the user explicitly says to add them.

Statuses: `todo`, `in-progress`, `blocked`, `done`, `needs-review`.
